Discover the Beauty of El Nido

El Nido is one of the most breathtaking places in the Philippines, located in the northern part of Palawan. It is known for its clear blue waters, tall limestone cliffs, and beautiful white sand beaches. This tropical paradise is filled with hidden lagoons, secret beaches, and colorful coral reefs that attract travelers from around the world.

Each destination in El Nido offers a unique experience — you can relax and swim at 7 Commandos Beach, explore the stunning rock formations and turquoise waters of the Big Lagoon, or kayak through the peaceful Small Lagoon on Miniloc Island. You can also visit Shimizu Island, a popular spot for snorkeling and diving where you can see vibrant marine life up close.

Whether you want to unwind, go on an island-hopping tour, or simply enjoy nature’s beauty, El Nido is the perfect destination that captures the true paradise of the Philippines.

Top Destinations in El Nido

From pristine lagoons to hidden beaches, explore the breathtaking beauty of El Nido.

7 Commandos Beach

A long stretch of powdery white sand lined with coconut trees — perfect for swimming and relaxing under the sun.

Big Lagoon

Famous for its crystal-clear turquoise waters surrounded by dramatic limestone cliffs, ideal for kayaking and sightseeing.

Small Lagoon

Known for its peaceful emerald-green waters where you can kayak through narrow rock openings.

Shimizu Island

A popular snorkeling site filled with vibrant coral reefs and colorful fish — great for beginner snorkelers.
Snake Island

Features a natural sandbar that connects two islands — best visited during low tide for a unique walking experience.
Cadlao Island

The largest island in El Nido, offering peaceful lagoons and hidden beaches with a stunning view of the bay.

Entalulua Island

A serene spot known for its calm waters and rich underwater world — a hidden gem for snorkeling lovers.
Pinagbuyutan Island

Recognized for its towering limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters, a favorite stop for island-hopping tours.

Secret Lagoon

A hidden gem accessible through a small rock opening, leading to a peaceful lagoon surrounded by cliffs.

Matinloc Island

A peaceful sanctuary on Matinloc Island offering panoramic views, a historical shrine, and a hidden beach nearby.

Nacpan Beach

A 4-kilometer paradise of golden sand and gentle waves, known as one of the most beautiful beaches in the world.
