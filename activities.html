Explore El Nido Island-Hopping Tours

Island hopping is the best way to discover El Nido’s beauty — from hidden lagoons and secret beaches to vibrant coral reefs. Choose from the popular tour packages below, each offering a unique tropical experience.

Tour A

Visit famous spots like the Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Shimizu Island, and Seven Commandos Beach — perfect for swimming, kayaking, and snorkeling.

Tour B

Explore caves and coves like Snake Island, Cudugnon Cave, Cathedral Cave, and Entalula Beach — a mix of adventure and relaxation.

Tour C

Discover hidden gems like Secret Beach, Hidden Beach, Helicopter Island, and Matinloc Shrine — ideal for adventure seekers.

Tour D

Visit the beautiful Cadlao Island and its beaches — Paradise Beach, Pasandigan Beach, and Nat Nat Beach — for a peaceful island experience.

Things to Do in El Nido

Kayaking in El Nido

Experience the beauty of El Nido from a new perspective as you paddle through its calm, turquoise waters. One of the best spots for kayaking is the Big Lagoon, where you can glide between towering limestone cliffs and explore hidden corners of the lagoon. You can also kayak around Cadlao Island, known for its peaceful surroundings and crystal-clear waters — perfect for a relaxing yet adventurous escape in paradise.

Snorkeling in El Nido

Discover the vibrant marine life beneath El Nido’s waters through snorkeling. Swim above colorful coral reefs and spot schools of tropical fish, sea turtles, and other fascinating sea creatures. Popular snorkeling spots like Shimizu Island and Seven Commandos Beach offer crystal-clear visibility and an unforgettable underwater adventure.

Hike to Taraw Peak

Get ready for an exciting adventure! Hiking Taraw Peak in El Nido offers a short but steep climb that rewards you with one of the most breathtaking sunrise views in Palawan. The trail begins in a small neighborhood before leading through the jungle and up sharp limestone rocks — perfect for those who love a challenge.

Though the climb can be humid and the rocks are jagged, the path is doable for most hikers. Once you reach the summit, you’ll be rewarded with a stunning panoramic view overlooking El Nido town, the turquoise bay, and the nearby islands — a truly unforgettable experience.

Visit Nacpan Beach by Motorbike

Explore the beauty of Nacpan Beach by renting a motorbike for a fun and easy ride from El Nido town. It’s just about 30 minutes away and offers one of the most stunning stretches of coastline in Palawan. The crystal-clear waters, swaying coconut trees, and cozy seaside cafés make it a perfect spot to unwind.

You can stroll along the peaceful shore, enjoy fresh seafood — especially shrimp — and sip on a refreshing mango smoothie while admiring the breathtaking view. Nacpan Beach truly captures the tropical paradise vibe that makes El Nido unforgettable.

Surfing in Duli Beach, El Nido

A hidden gem in El Nido, Duli Beach is perfect for surfing or simply relaxing by the shore. With golden sand, rolling waves, and peaceful sunset views, it’s an ideal escape for those who love adventure and tranquility.

Lunch on the Island

During your island-hopping adventure in El Nido, you can enjoy a freshly cooked lunch right on the beach. Most tours serve grilled seafood, chicken, pork, rice, and fresh fruits — all prepared by your boat crew. Imagine eating a delicious meal under the shade of palm trees, with your feet in the sand and a beautiful ocean view all around. It’s a true island experience you’ll never forget.
